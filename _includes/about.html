About $30 wind turbine

Works in winds from 15-100km/h (we've tested it on the back of a holden commodore). The only materials you need for this is some old aluminium printing plates, a bike wheel, and some rivets. You really need to write this yourself

The whole thing takes about 4 hours to put together with just a ruler, a box-cutter, a drill, and a rivet-gun.
